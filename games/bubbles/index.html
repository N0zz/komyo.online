Bubble Pop

Shoot glossy bubbles to pop groups of 3+ — Arcade, Endless and Zen, with special shots.

How to play

Aim the cannon, bounce shots off the walls, and match three or more bubbles of a color to pop them — drop whole clusters for big combos. Special shots clear tough spots. Arcade climbs level by level, Endless pushes the ceiling down forever, and Zen just lets you pop.
