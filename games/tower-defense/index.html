Keep Defender

Hold the path. Build towers, spend gold, defend the keep wave after wave.

How to play

Enemies march the path toward your keep — build archer, cannon, frost and mage towers beside it to stop them. Each kill pays gold; place and upgrade between waves, and switch targeting (first, strongest, weakest) to match the threat. Pick a map and difficulty, survive every wave to win.
