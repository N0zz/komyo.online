Minesweeper

Clear the minefield — flag bombs, read the numbers, deduce the rest. The logic classic.

How to play

Reveal tiles and read the numbers — each one counts the mines touching it. Flag the mines you've deduced (long-press on touch), and chord a satisfied number to clear its neighbors at once. Your first dig is always safe; sweep the whole field without a boom to win. Relaxed mode gives you three hearts.
