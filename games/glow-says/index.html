Glow Says

Watch the lights, repeat the tune — how long a sequence can you remember?

How to play

The lanterns glow in a sequence — repeat it by tapping them in the same order, and each round adds one more step. Chill mode never ends your run on a slip, Classic is the pure test, Hard adds two extra lanterns and Expert packs nine into a grid. Sound on helps: every lantern sings its own note.

Can I play offline? Yes — after your first visit the game works without internet, and you can install it as an app straight from your browser.

Where are my scores saved? On your device only — best scores, trophies and cosmetics live in your browser and are never uploaded anywhere.
