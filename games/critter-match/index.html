Critter Match

Flip the cards, find the matching animals. Grows with you, gentle and cheerful.

How to play

Flip two cards a turn and remember what you saw — match the animal pairs to clear the board in as few flips as you can. Boards grow from a quick 4×3 to a challenge grid, Speedrun races the clock, and Pass & Play deals the turns out to 2–4 players on one screen.
