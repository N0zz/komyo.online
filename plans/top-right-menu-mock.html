What changed from the last round

  • Fixed v8's actual bug: the tab and the panel were both inside one 22px clip window — and since content flows left-to-right from the window's left edge, the visible 22px strip was a sliver of the panel, while the tab (last in the row) was what got clipped away. At width 0 the panel is invisible by construction.
