Current vs Modern-produced. Left = today's engine (ported 1:1). Right = a fully-produced take, each game in its own style. Snake has two modern takes to compare — a faithful remaster of today's tune, and a from-scratch electro banger. Asteroids is a pumping space-rave, Breakout half-time synthwave, Keep Defender epic taikos + choir, Meadow lush swells.

How to listen: click a cell to play (again to stop). On the Modern cell, drag Intensity or hit Build / Peak — it adds & drops layers, opens the filter and changes the beat as intensity rises; Auto-ramp makes it breathe on its own, like a wave ramping up. Current doesn't react.
